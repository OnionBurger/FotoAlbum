QWEASD - rotate pic
R - rotation reset
Z - prev pic
X/ENTER - next pic
C/SPACE - pause
V - change projection
F - toggle fullscreen

****

config format:
	folder with pics
	0 (alphabetical) ili 1 (random)
	duration of pic
	duration of trans
	+transName for enter (multiple allowed, each in single line)
	-transName for exit (multiple allowed, each in single line)

****

transition names:
	sudden
	wipe
	fade
	spin
	clock
	ellipsoid
	bring
	cone
